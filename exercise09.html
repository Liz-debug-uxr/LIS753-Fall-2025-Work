Friends of Palo Alto Library

Monthly Used Book Sale

Find great books, music, videos, and more while supporting your local library!

About the Sale

We have over 50,000 wonderful used books, records, videos, magazines, and more at our monthly sale. Items come from donations by individuals, estates, and companies, as well as items the Palo Alto Library has removed from their shelves to make room for new purchases.

Main Room

The Main Room, located near the Charleston Shopping Center, features hardbound books starting at $1 and paperbacks at 50 cents. Large book donations should be brought here as well.

Children's Books, Music, and More

In our new room near the Cubberley track and Greendell School, you'll find children's books, music, videos, and toys priced at 50 cents to $1. This includes picture books, school-age fiction, nonfiction, and more.

Bargain Room

The Bargain Room (Rooms K6 and K7) offers hardbound books for $1, paperbacks for 50 cents, and children's books for just 25 cents. Prices drop by half on Sunday, and you can buy a grocery bag for $5 to fill with books.

Important Customer Information

  • Gift certificates available in $5 denominations.
  • Maximum two numbered tickets per person at the Main Room, starting at 8 am on Saturdays.
  • Only 160 customers allowed in the Main Room at a time due to space limitations.
